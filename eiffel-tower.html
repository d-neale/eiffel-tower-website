La Tour Eiffel

Paris, France

Constructed 1887-1889

Find the Eiffel Tower here:

The construction schedule

  1. The first floor was completed on the 1st April 1888
  2. The second floor was completed on the 14th August 1888
  3. The top section was completed on the 31st March 1889

Facts and figures

  • It was the world's tallest building at 1,024 feet (312.11 meters) when it opened
  • It has 1,665 stairs and three viewing platforms
  • It has 120 antennas and 50 miles of electric cables
